ChrisG

Christopher Gonzalez

Chris G is arguably one of the most storied Fighting Game players in the U.S., if not the world. His prowess as a FG player is unquestioned and features a list of major tournament wins that is too long to count. Described as one of the best Ultimate Marvel vs Capcom 3 (UMVC3) players in the world, Chris is well known for his ability to take an impossible situation and turn it into a win.

Currently Chris is playing Ultimate Marvel Vs. Capcom 3, Ultra Street Fighter 4, Mortal Combat X, and many others.
