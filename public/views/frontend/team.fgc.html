ChrisG

Christopher Gonzalez

Chris G is arguably one of the most storied Fighting Game players in the U.S., if not the world. His prowess as a FG player is unquestioned and features a list of major tournament wins that is too long to count. Described as one of the best Ultimate Marvel vs Capcom 3 (UMVC3) players in the world, Chris is well known for his ability to take an impossible situation and turn it into a win.

Currently Chris is playing Ultimate Marvel Vs. Capcom 3, Ultra Street Fighter 4, Mortal Kombat X, and many others.

Rip

Reepal Parbhoo

I'm Reepal "Rip" Parbhoo and I've been involved in the fighting game community since the late 90's. I've competed since 2005 with my main accomplishment being Top 3 at Evolution 3 years in a row for Tekken 6 and getting to compete internationally for that game as well. Aside from competing I've cast: Tekken, Injustice, and Killer Instinct for MLG and Evolution over the years. As a strategy guide author for Prima Games, I had the opportunity to co-author the Tekken Tag 2, Killer Instinct, and Smash 4 strategy guides. I look to use these experiences, plus my attention to detail, to help Tempo Storm's FGC Team grow and prosper!
