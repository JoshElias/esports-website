ryx

Ronnie Bylicki

Ryx is the current leader of Tempo Storm’s CS:GO team and a regular streamer on Twitch.tv. Ryx entered the competitive Counter Strike scene in 2006 and has since made a name for himself in ESEA. During his time in competitive CS:GO he has won the ESEA-Main competition twice, placed second in ESEA-main, and won a ESEA-open while remaining undefeated. Ryx fills the role of fragger and secondary In Game leader (IGL).

Ryx often describes his approach to professional gaming with the following quote "Be humble, hungry, and always be the hardest worker in the room."

Stanislaw

Peter Jarguz

Stanislaw first began playing competitive CS in 2012 towards the end of the 1.6 era. Upon the release of CSGO, he slowly transitioned and adapted to the new style of gameplay and began competing competitively once again in 2014. This was a standout year for Stanislaw, as he led his former team to an ESEA-IM championship, a 2nd place finish at the ESWC Montreal LAN Qualifier, as well as an invite to the first ever ESEA-P division. It was during this season where he established himself as one of the most consistent players in the division.

It wasn’t long before he was approached by an ESEA-Invite team, where he gained valuable experience against some of the best talent in North America. Stanislaw’s stint in Invite only lasted a short while, and soon after joined forces with ryx & company in ESEA-P, helping them achieve 1st place with an undefeated record of 19-0. Now Stanislaw is once again at the highest level of competitive CSGO, competing against the best players in North America with team Tempo Storm.

ShahZaM

ShahZeb Khan

ShahZeb "ShahZaM" Khan is well known in the CS:GO community for his mastery of the AWP. He has been a consistent competitive figure in Counter-Strike for well over 7 years. ShahZaM’s extensive experience in CS:GO has allowed him to compete on a variety of professional teams and is a testament to his enduring skill at the game.

Roca

Daniel Gustaferri

Danny “Roca” Gustaferri has a long history in competitive FPS games going back to 2010. Roca’s excels as an entry fragger and is known for his ability to turn the tide of a battle with his quick aiming skills. In his first match as a Tempo Storm member he was instrumental in overcoming one of North America’s best CS:GO teams at the time and demonstrates his ability to adjust to the changing dynamics of the game.

Warden

Matt Dickens

Matt “Warden” Dickens needs no introduction for veterans of the CS community. His time as a competitive CS player marked the reemergence of NA as a powerful contender on the international FPS stage. His time on the professional scene brought with it a staggering amount of competitive wins and showcased his abilities as a player. As a coach, Warden brings with him the experience of not only a professional top tier player, but also the understanding of what it takes for a team to break into the international scene.
