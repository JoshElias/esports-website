Stanislaw

Peter Jarguz

Stanislaw first began playing competitive CS in 2012 towards the end of the 1.6 era. Upon the release of CSGO, he slowly transitioned and adapted to the new style of gameplay and began competing competitively once again in 2014. This was a standout year for Stanislaw, as he led his former team to an ESEA-IM championship, a 2nd place finish at the ESWC Montreal LAN Qualifier, as well as an invite to the first ever ESEA-P division. It was during this season where he established himself as one of the most consistent players in the division.

It wasn’t long before he was approached by an ESEA-Invite team, where he gained valuable experience against some of the best talent in North America. Stanislaw’s stint in Invite only lasted a short while, and soon after joined forces with ryx & company in ESEA-P, helping them achieve 1st place with an undefeated record of 19-0. Now Stanislaw is once again at the highest level of competitive CSGO, competing against the best players in North America with team Tempo Storm.

sLip

Ricky Cohen

My role on the team is the entry fragger and secondary awper. I have been playing CS steadily since 2006 where I started as a pubber, and worked my ways through the ranks. I have always believed that hard work pays off, and will earn you respect as a player. When CS:GO came out I didn’t pick the game up right away, instead focused on other priorities in my life. For the first year I didn’t even log 10 hours total. As time went on I saw the potential of CS:GO and realized how much I enjoyed competing in the counter-strike franchise, so I dedicated myself to the game. At first, no one took me seriously and I struggled to find a team. I can’t stress enough to those trying to get into the competitive scene: hard work pays off. I earned my spot back playing on competitive teams through the time and effort I put back into the game. I’ve always been considered the “young gunner” on all of my teams, but now with CS:GO getting huge -- it’s the opposite. I’m considered the veteran player on the team who has tons of experience playing in super competitive / high stress matches. I keep the team under control and make sure that we stick it out to the end if we’re behind, and keeping everyone in check when we’re ahead to make sure we close out the match without getting antsy.
